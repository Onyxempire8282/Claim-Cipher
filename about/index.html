About Claim Cipher

Our Mission

Claim Cipher is a comprehensive tool designed for insurance adjusters to streamline the billing and route optimization process. With its user‑friendly interface and powerful features, Claim Cipher helps adjusters calculate mileage, estimate costs and optimize routes efficiently.

Features

  • Mileage Calculator
  • Route Optimization
  • Billing Estimator
  • Integration with Google Maps API
